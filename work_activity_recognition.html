Challenge

Activities are highly diverse, ranging from simple tasks like walking or sitting to complex actions like exercising or household chores. This diversity is amplified by differences among people, including variations in age, fitness levels, physical abilities, and lifestyle.
Additionally, sensor noise, differences in user behavior, and environmental factors can reduce accuracy, requiring robust models and large datasets for effective recognition.
